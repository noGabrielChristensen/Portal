Notes & Guidance

Welcome! This page contains guidance, progress updates, and tips for your website and projects. Content here will evolve as features are implemented and improvements are made.

Website Domain

Your official website domain is fully customizable. Domains must be purchased, typically costing $10–$15 per year. Administration manages all domain settings.

Testing & Updates

All updates and new features are first tested on a cloned version of the site to ensure stability before deployment to the live environment. This prevents disruptions and ensures reliability.

Music & Features

The music player allows uploading tracks and managing a local queue. Future updates will enable cloud saving and cross-device access.

What’s in Progress

The donation button is ready to link with your CashApp account. A secure admin message box for feature requests will be implemented soon.
